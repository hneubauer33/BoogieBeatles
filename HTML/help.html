Help!

August 6, 1965

“Paul, of course, had written his ‘Yesterday’; the most recorded song in history. What a guy!” - Ringo Starr

In Help! The Beatles start experimenting with new sounds and exploring their emotions. The album has influences of country and western, bluegrass, folk, classical and rock style. Lennon was beginning to dig deep into his own emotions, taking influences from Dylan while writing hits like “Ticket to Ride” and “You’ve got to hide your love away.” The album also includes the solo song Yesterday by Paul Mccartney. “Yesterday” is the most covered song of all time and has been performed an estimated 7 million times. For the photography, Freedman had the band pose in semaphore. Originally hoping they could spell out help, however, they ended up spelling ‘NUJV’ because it looked better.
