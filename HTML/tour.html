Magical Mystery Tour

November 27, 1967

“Magical Mystery Tour is one of my favourite albums, because it was so weird. ‘I Am The Walrus’ is also one of my favourite tracks – because I did it, of course, but also because it’s one of those that has enough little bitties going to keep you interested even a hundred years later.” - John Lennon

In wake of the death of Beatles Manager Brian Epstein, the band was for the first time without direction. They had decided to continue with The Magical Myster Tour a project they had started after Sgt. Pepper. McCartney often took on the role of manager, hoping to keep the band afloat during their time of mourning. The album was created along side a televsion special featuring the music from the album. The film, although slated by critics and the public, showed off their psychedelic experimentation.
